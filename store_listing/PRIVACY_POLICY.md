# Privacy Policy for Liquid 2048

**Last Updated: December 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to Liquid 2048 ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application Liquid 2048 (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Information We DO NOT Collect

Liquid 2048 is designed with your privacy in mind. We do **NOT** collect:

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers for advertising
- Usage analytics or behavioral data
- Photos, contacts, or any other personal files
- Payment information

### Information Stored Locally

The App stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- **High Score**: Your best game score is saved locally to track your progress
- **Game State**: Current game progress is saved so you can continue where you left off

This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not transmitted to any servers
- Is not shared with any third parties
- Can be deleted by uninstalling the App

## Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device using standard platform storage mechanisms:
- **iOS**: UserDefaults
- **Android**: SharedPreferences

No cloud storage or external servers are used.

## Third-Party Services

Liquid 2048 does **NOT** use any third-party services such as:
- Analytics platforms
- Advertising networks
- Social media integrations
- Crash reporting services

## Children's Privacy

Liquid 2048 is suitable for all ages and does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The App complies with:
- Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

## Data Security

Since we don't collect or transmit any personal data, there are no security risks associated with data breaches or unauthorized access to your information through our App.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Delete your data**: Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data
- **Access your data**: Game scores are visible within the App

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
